Research talks at top AI and sports analytics conferences.

AAAI 2025

Optimising Spatial Teamwork Under Uncertainty

Research on a novel method for assessing agent teamwork based on spatial coordination using Monte Carlo Tree Search and linear programming. Applied to optimising team defensive structures in football.

MIT Sloan 2024

The Strain of Success: A Predictive Model for Injury Mitigation and Team Success

Research on the use of machine learning and reinforcement learning-based search methods to optimise team selections over a season to maximise team success while mitigating player injuries.

Watch talk
StatsBomb 2022

Contextual Expected Threat using Spatial Event Data

Research on using spatial event data to provide additional context to an expected threat model to compute goal-scoring probabilities, and how this can be leveraged to evaluate team positioning in attack and defence with a novel TaX metric.
